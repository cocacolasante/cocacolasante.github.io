Applying for a grant?

We CAN HELP!

At NPO Grant Writing, we will prepare high-quality grant proposals for state, federal, local, and private funding opportunities for non-profits.

We at NPO Grant Writing are passionate about helping non-profit organizations maximize their funding opportunities so that they can continue doing what they do best: providing life-changing support to the members of their communities in need.

Personalized Touch

As a small team of skilled writers, we only work with a select few non-profits at a time, providing a personalized experience that cultivates a deep understanding of the organizations we partner with, which is essential to the quality and success of our grant writing services.

Personalized Results

Our process has resulted in our services being contracted annually to accommodate multiple non-profit grant cycles, which we believe non-profits should never miss out on. However, we are also happy to work with clients on one-time projects.

Setting Your Non-Profit Up For Success

All writing and research pertaining to grant opportunities will become the property of your non-profit organization, providing you re-usable materials for future grant opportunities as well as marketing efforts. Whether you require a long-term partnership or a one-time service, NPO Grant Writing can help your non-profit achieve and maintain excellence.

Every non-profit is different.

We believe in tailoring our services to the needs and goals of each individual client. Call/email for an informational meeting and cost proposal for services offered.
